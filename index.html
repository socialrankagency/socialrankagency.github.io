Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Still deciding? Here's what most clients ask before we start.

Do you only work with businesses in Dera Ghazi Khan?+

No — we handle business worldwide; our physical location is only Pakistan (Dera Ghazi Khan). We design, develop and market for clients across Pakistan and internationally. Most communication happens over call, WhatsApp and email.

Will my website be fully coded, not a template?+

Yes. Every website we deliver is hand-coded in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, built specifically around your brand and content — not a recycled theme with your logo dropped in.

Which platforms and technologies do you work with?+

We build custom sites from scratch and also work within WordPress, Shopify and other platforms when that's the better fit — we'll recommend whichever suits your goals and budget.

What's included in the free audit?+

We review your current website's speed, structure and SEO health, check where you currently rank for key search terms, and send you a short report showing the biggest opportunities.

How much does a website or SEO package cost?+

Pricing depends on scope — a brochure site differs from a full e-commerce build or an ongoing SEO campaign. Share your requirements on a discovery call and we'll send a clear, itemised quote.

Do you offer monthly retainers or one-time projects?+

Both. Websites are typically a one-time build with optional maintenance, while SEO and digital marketing work best as an ongoing monthly retainer so results keep compounding.

How long does it take to see SEO results?+

Most clients start seeing measurable movement in rankings within 6-10 weeks, with stronger, compounding results by month 4-6. SEO is ongoing — the longer it runs, the harder it is for competitors to catch up.

How do I get started?+

Reach out through the contact page or WhatsApp — we'll set up a short discovery call, understand your goals, and follow up with a clear plan and quote.

What happens after my website goes live?+

We hand over a walkthrough of your site, stay on for support and small fixes, and — if you're running SEO or marketing with us — move straight into monthly reporting and optimisation.
